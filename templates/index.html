The Company



We are registered Property Manager under the Valuers, Appraisals, Estate Agents and Property Management Act 1981.

Established in the year 2011, we have been providing Building and Facilities Management Service for Gated & Guarded Community, High Rise Building and Commercial Property.

Our top management team consists of professionals in the field of Finance, Human Capital Management and Facilities Management with hands on experience in managing Buildings governed under Strata Management.

Since our expertise is diverse and varied, we are able to map our strategies for the Property as an investment as well as enhancing its prestige and value.

We are capable of managing a wide spectrum of Property & Facilities Mnagement, encompassing Building Maintenance, Finance, Rules & Regulation and Human Relations effectively.

We are well verse and always keep abreast on the latest development on Strata Management and any new legislation that may be introduced from time to time, to manage efficiently the legal aspect of Property Management.
